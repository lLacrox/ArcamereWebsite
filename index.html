Mission Statement

Arcamere is a collaborative, world-building focused roleplay Minecraft server. We are creating a cohesive and interesting story that reaches across in-game borders, for all players to enjoy. The framework is simple: create your civilization, build your lore, shape history, and weave memories worth remembering.
